About Me

I was born and raised in the most beautiful city in the world, Charleston, South Carolina (see background above). Since 2017 I have lived in Provo. I have an older brother, Davey (35), and sister, Leslie (32). Both of my siblings are married and I have a niece and a nephew who are as cute as can be. My parents still live in the house I grew up in.

I am a junior at BYU right now, and I will be applying to the Information Systems major soon. Technology has always been an interest of mine, however, my goal is to be a lawyer specializing in cybersecurity law. After graduating from BYU I plan to go to law school somewhere closer to home.

My favorite teams are the Carolina Panthers, because that's the team from home, and the Cincinnati Bengals, because my dad is from Cincinnati and would always watch their games when I was little.

One other thing about me...I love Star Wars! I have seen all the movies and TV shows, and I have started reading some of the books. My favorite character in the Star Wars universe is Anakin Skywalker.
